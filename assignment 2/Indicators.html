The diagram below shows examples of indicators


COMMODITY CHANNEL INDEX(CCI)

 Importance of CCI
  • CCI helps to identify volatility in a currecy pair
  • Helps you to identify potential reversal points
  • Helps you to confirm the strength of a currency
Entry & Exist signals
  • When the CCI rises above 100 and turns crossing back 100 you can SELL
  • When the CCI rises above 0 you can BUY












MOVING AVERAGE CONVERDENCE DIVERGENCE (MACD)

 Importance of MACD
  • MACD helps to identify when the momentum of a currency pair changes
  • It helps to confirm the strength of a currency trends
Entry & Exist
  • When the MACD crosses above the trigger line you can BUY knowing momentum has shifted from bearish to bullish
  • When the MACD crosses below the line you can SELL knowing that momentum has shifted
















BOLLINGER BANDS

 Importance of Bollinger Bands
  • They help you identify the trend
  • They identify current market volatility
Entry & Exist
  • ENTER when the Bands widen and begin moving in opposite direction after a period of consolidation you can eitheir BUY or SELL in the direction the price is moving
  • EXIST when the bands narrow
